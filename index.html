profile


SmartShell Electronics is a general practionner entreprise founded in Sherbrooke, Quebec. The entreprise's domain of expertise is mostly PCB design and firmware development. Target customers are companies, groups or individuals who are looking to make a step forward in their projects in terms of electronics. SmartShell Electronics is looking as much for small short-term projects as important long-term product development. Consulting services for projects needing advice and re-organization are also offered.
